Bhagavad Gita - Chapter 1: The Yoga of Arjuna's Dejection

Verse 1.1:
Dhritarashtra said: O Sanjaya, what did my sons and the sons of Pandu do when they assembled on the sacred land of Kurukshetra, eager for battle?

Verse 1.2:
Sanjaya said: Seeing the army of the Pandavas arrayed for battle, King Duryodhana approached his teacher Drona and spoke these words.

Verse 1.3:
Behold, O Teacher, this mighty army of the sons of Pandu, arrayed by your intelligent disciple, the son of Drupada.
